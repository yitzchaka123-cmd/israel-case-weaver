

## Control who can sign in: invite code + admin approval (best of both)

You actually want **both** mechanisms — they solve different problems:

- **Invite code** = friction at the door. Stops random Google users from even creating an account.
- **Admin approval** = you stay in control even if someone *has* the code (shared, leaked, guessed). Nothing in the app works until you click "Approve".

Either alone has a hole. Combined, they're bulletproof and still take ~5 seconds for a legitimate invitee.
